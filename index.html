The 14th International Joint Conference on Knowledge Graphs (IJCKG 2025) is a premium academic forum on Knowledge Graphs. The mission of IJCKG 2025 is to bring together international researchers in the Knowledge Graph community and other related areas to present their innovative research results or novel applications of Knowledge Graphs. IJCKG has evolved from the Joint International Semantic Technology Conference (JIST): a joint event for disseminating research results regarding the Semantic Web, Knowledge Graphs, Linked Data and AI on the Web.

The 14th International Joint Conference on Knowledge Graphs (IJCKG 2025) will take place from September X to Y, 2025 in FORTH, the Foundation for Research and Technology - Hellas, Heraklion, Greece.
