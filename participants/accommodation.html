Accommodations

Heraklion offers a wide variety of accommodation options to suit different preferences and needs. In the heart of the city, visitors can find numerous hotels that provide convenient access to historical landmarks, restaurants, and vibrant local markets. These centrally located hotels are ideal for conference participants who wish to explore the city’s attractions within walking distance or enjoy the lively atmosphere of the city center. For those seeking a more tranquil and scenic stay, there are several hotels and resorts located along the coast, just a short distance from the city. The conference takes place toward the end of the tourist season, a period when the island begins to transition from its peak summer months to a quieter, more serene ambiance. As a result, many hotels—both in the city and by the beach—are less crowded, offering a more relaxed experience and often more competitive rates.
