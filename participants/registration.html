Registration Details

Registration for Authors

For each accepted paper (for each track: research, in-use, track, demo, poster, workshop, evaluation challenge), at least one author needs to have a regular (i.e., non-student) registration. This regular registration should be done before Sept 7, 2025, to have their paper included in the conference proceedings. If you have multiple accepted papers, each paper must have a separate regular (i.e., non-student) registration.

Registration fees

Early Bird (until Sept 7, 2025) 350 €200 €

*: Student registrations include everything included in the corresponding non-student registrations, however students will be requested to upload a valid student card or a corresponding student certificate during the registration process.

Covered Services

Regular and student registration types include:
  • Admission to a very strong technical program comprising technical sessions, keynotes, tutorials, and specialized workshops.
  • Coffee breaks from Wed Oct 15 to Fri Oct 17.
  • Lunches from from Wed Oct 15 to Fri Oct 17.
For those registered only as presenters at the PhD/Post-doctoral symposium, lunches are not included.

How to register (in 4 simple steps)

  1. Create Account - Create an EasyConferences.org account: [Create Account]
  2. Verify email - Click on the activation link sent to your email
  3. Log In - After verifying your email, login to EasyConferences.org by clicking the folowing link: [Log In]
  4. Create Order - Create and settle your order online securely: [Order and Register]

Cancellation Policy

The registrations are binding and non-refundable. According to sustainability principles and to avoid food waste, please cancel your registration if you cannot attend the conference or the social event. Participation that has already been registered can be transferred to another person from the same organisation without any extra charge.

Visa Information

Depending on the duration and purpose of your journey to Greece, you may apply for a short-term “Schengen visa”. Before applying for a visa please make sure that you have read carefully the information on Applying for a Schengen visa.

For any inquires regarding visa supporting letters, please contact IJCKG 2025 local organizers (fafalios 'AT' ics.forth.gr, mountant 'AT' ics.forth.gr)
