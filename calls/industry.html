Call for Industry Forum

IJCKG 2025 will organize an Industry Forum related to Knowledge Graphs and Large Language Models. The Industry Forum is designed to bring together industrial stakeholders, technology providers and practitioners working with knowledge graphs to:

  • Showcase recent technological advances, products and services
  • Share real-world experiences, challenges and lessons learned
  • Present future directions and collaboration opportunities
  • Foster synergies with academia and other industrial players

Each particant will be given the opportunity to:

  • Deliver a 10-minute presentation during the Industry Forum session
  • Display a poster throughout the conference to further promote their work

We welcome contributions from companies of all sizes, including startups, SMEs, and large enterprises that are active in the field of knowledge graphs, data management, semantics and large language models.

How to participate
To express your interest in participating in the Industry Forum, please contact the organizing committee with the following information:

  • Company name and website
  • Name, affiliation and short bio of presenter
  • Title and short abstract of the proposed presentation
  • Please submit the proposal to the email: carter.whfcarter 'AT' gmail.com
  • Express your interest by August 1, 2025
