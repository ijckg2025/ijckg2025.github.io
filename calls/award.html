IJCKG 2025 recognizes outstanding contributions to the field of knowledge graphs and large language models by introducing two awards:

Best Paper Award
This award will be presented to the author(s) of the most outstanding paper, selected by the Program Committee based on originality, technical quality, and relevance to the conference themes.

Best Student Paper Award
This award aims to encourage and honor high-quality research conducted by students. It will be given to the best paper primarily authored by a student, who must also present the paper at the conference.

Both awards will be announced during the closing session of the conference.
