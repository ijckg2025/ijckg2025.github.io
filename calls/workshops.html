Call for Workshops

IJCKG 2025 cordially invites you to submit workshop proposals to be held in conjunction with the conference. The workshops will be held in parallel with the conference, and the maximum length will be two hours. The purpose of a workshop is to provide an opportunity for participants for an in-depth discussion of current and emerging topics of semantic technologies. Ideally, a workshop should foster interactions between different communities within the scope of the IJCKG conference.

The organizers of the accepted workshops will be responsible for the reviewing process, call for papers, publicity website, and proceeding publication of the workshop.

Workshop proposal should include the following information:

  • The title and abstract, 300 words maximum, for inclusion on the IJCKG 2025 website.
  • Topics of interest.
  • A list of organizers for the workshop with their brief Bio(s).
  • Your preference regarding the length of the workshop: (half day - full day)
Proposals should be sent via email to workship chairs ( kondylak@ics.forth.gr & m.navas@upm.es )
